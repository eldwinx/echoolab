SUMMARY

Our task was to create a short animation using the knowledge that we learned throughout the semester. We decided to create a comedy short called Oops. It is about two domino pieces woke up at late night and start playing around and one accidentally pushed over the other. After realizing what just happened, they witnessed the whole room was going down with a "domino effect".
